🍩 About Us 🍩

Smash Baked Coffeehouse was created for people who believe coffee and cookies should be fun. We bake big, colorful treats, serve cheerful drinks, and create a space where friends, families, students, and dessert lovers can relax, laugh, and enjoy something sweet.

Whether you are grabbing a latte before work or treating yourself to a giant cookie, every visit should feel like a small celebration.
